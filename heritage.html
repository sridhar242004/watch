Our Heritage

A legacy of excellence since 1875

1875

Foundation of CHRONOS by master watchmaker Alexandre Chronos in Geneva, Switzerland.

1925

Introduction of the revolutionary self-winding mechanism, setting new standards in watchmaking.

1950

Launch of the iconic Celestial collection, featuring groundbreaking astronomical complications.

1975

CHRONOS introduces the world's thinnest automatic perpetual calendar watch.

2000

Opening of the CHRONOS Museum, showcasing our rich history and technological innovations.
